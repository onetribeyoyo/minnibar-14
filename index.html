programming vs engineering

When building just about anything...

remember... programming vs engineering

Engineering Process


  1. Research,

  2. Prototype,

  3. Sensitivity analysis (what matters?),

  4. Final build.

Command & Control
(waterfall-ish)


  1. Research
    "decide everything up-front,"
  2. Prototype
    "shutup and just build it,"
  3. Sensitivity analysis
    "sorry, it's too late to fix anything",
  4. Final build
    "find another job 'cause a lot of people
    are pissed off".

Agile(-ish)


  1. Research
    "product backlog,"
  2. Prototype
    "MVP,"
  3. Sensitivity analysis
    "prioritization and retrospectives",
  4. Final build
